Detailed Description

This message is sent by the local requesting Radio to its Host at the end of a full UWB ranging conversation to report filtered range info.

◆ rangeStatus

rcrm_uint8_t rcmMsg_FilteredRangeInfo::rangeStatus

• 0 - Success: range measurement was completed with no issues.

• 1 - Timeout - never received the range response

• 3 - Virtual Carrier Sense - indicates that another ongoing range conversation would have been interrupted by our range attempt, so no range measurement was attempted.

• 5, 7, 9, 11, 13, 15 are all reserved, internal error codes

• 17 - CRC Mismatch - the UWB range response packet was received at the expected time, but failed its CRC check.

• 19 - reserved, internal error code

Completed range conversations where a measurement issue was detected might have one or more of the following bits set.

• 2 (Bit 1) - Requester LED Failure - Indicates the requesting radio could not determine the exact timing of the pulse for range measurement. • 4 (Bit 2) - Responder LED Failure - Indicates the responding radio could not determine the exact timing of the pulse for range measurement.

• 32 (Bit 5) - Range Out of Bounds - Indicates the range calculation produced a bogus result.
